Mandate

Where we deploy capital.

We invest selectively across three complementary disciplines, each chosen for its capacity to compound over decades — not quarters.

i.

Real assets

Direct positions in income-producing real estate, alongside operators with proven discipline. Buy and hold.

ii.

Operating businesses

Significant minority or majority equity in private companies generating durable cash flow. Boring, profitable, well-run.

iii.

Growth-stage technology

Selective checks into companies building infrastructure, fintech and AI-native tooling. Capital efficiency over hype.

iv.

Co-investments

Selective participation alongside trusted GPs and operating partners we have worked with for years.

v.

Special situations

Bespoke transactions where structure, speed and discretion create asymmetric outcomes for both sides.

vi.

Founder partnerships

We work alongside operators we believe in — for the long arc of a business, not the next financing round.
